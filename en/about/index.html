For over 50 years the Beatty family has provided the food processing and packaging market in the Caribbean region, the best fully vertical integrated production solutions: Meat, Poultry, Seafood and Bakery. Throughout this journey, the top manufacturers in the industry have tapped the Beatty’s for market representation and growth. We have sealed long-term relations with the top companies in our market, consistently fulfilling production strategies and goals.


Trident Caribbean LLC represents the new millennia, the evolution of the Beatty family business, guiding clients and prospects with better products, services and who seek growth and meet capacity: The introduction of Sumidom, SA, Trident's subsidiary in the Dominican Republic, delivering top quality brands to this growing local market.


We continually strive to be better, adding new lines and penetrating new markets, Jamaica, Barbados, Trinidad & Tobago... Trident Caribbean, LLC, a full service distributor, bringing the best-in- class brands to your doorstep!
